## 🌟 About

**Learning-Islam-Telegram-Bot** is a free, open-source Telegram bot created to help Muslims (and anyone interested) learn more about Islam in an interactive, convenient, and authentic way — right inside Telegram.

Whether you're a complete beginner or looking to deepen your knowledge, the bot offers bite-sized, structured content drawn from reliable Islamic sources.

### Core Features
- **Quran Lookup** — Search by surah/ayah number, keyword, or juz' — with Arabic text + translation
- **Hadith Search** — Browse authentic collections (Bukhari, Muslim, etc.) by book, number, or topic
- **Prayer Time Reminders** — Location-based salah times + athan notifications (optional)
- **Islamic Quizzes** — Fun, educational tests on Aqeedah, Fiqh, Seerah, Quran, and more
- **Structured Lessons** — Beginner-friendly modules covering basics of Islam, step by step
- **Daily Reminders** (optional) — Ayah/Hadith of the day, morning & evening adhkar, etc.
- Inline mode support (search without leaving your chat)
- Multi-language support (planned / contributions welcome!)
